About Me

☺️My name is Lin Yuxuan, born on November 16, 2001, from Nanjing, Jiangsu Province. My MBTI is INFP-a type. I have a passion for reading, movie watching, photography and taking walks on streets ......

👩🏻‍🎓I studied at Nanjing Audit University for my undergraduate degree, majoring in auditing. However, my dream from high school was to study journalism and communication, so I passed the inter-specialty examination in graduate school and got admitted to Nanjing University, majoring in journalism and communication, and my research direction is digital marketing communication.

😻I will keep exploring my career development in the future, and find a career that I love and am willing to work hard for, and I hope to be able to bring beauty to the world through my own efforts.

🌲 By the way, my friends usually call me Xiao Lin or Xiao Shu (small tree in Chinese). I love the imagery of a tree, and I hope that I can be as natural and free to grow as it symbolizes... This is also the reason why I call my website 'Treehouse'☺️！
